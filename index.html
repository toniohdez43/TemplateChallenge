Rhubarb: The Vegetable That Acts Like a Fruit

May is all about vegetables on bobbyflay.com and one of the most popular springtime vegetables ir rhubarb. That's right, I said vegetable. Did you know that rhubarb is actually a vegetable, not a fruit?

While rhubarb is typically treated like a fruit and most often shows up in desserts in this country, it is also very good in savory dishes too and when used in glazed and chutneys pairs really well with pork, lamb and chicken. But, since I could live on pastries (and often do) I love using rhubarb in a variety of desserts.
